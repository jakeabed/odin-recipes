Cheeseburger

Cheeseburgers are often built up into complicated affair, but there is a beauty in simplicity. An ideal cheeseburger features a smash patty, pickles, onions, cheese, any desired sauces, and a bun. Obviously you can modify to your heart's desire, but the cheeseburger's heart is best maintained with little fuss.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef - 115g Per Patty
  • Hamburger Buns
  • American or Cheddar Cheese
  • Sliced Dill Pickle Rounds
  • Diced White Onions
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Ketchup, Mayonaise, Mustard, & Any Other Toppings You Desire

Directions

  1. Shape each portion of beef into a round ball.
  2. Add some oil to a searing hot pan or griddle.
  3. Drop your patty onto the pan or griddle and smash it flat with a spatula or similar implement. Try to be quick without damaging the integrity of the patty.
  4. Quickly season the patty with a little salt and fresh pepper.
  5. After one minute, flip the patty. Immediately place a slice of cheese on the patty. Let it cook for one more minute, then remove from the heat.
  6. Turn the heat on the pan down to medium, then place your buns inside-down on the hot, greasy pan to toast them. One to two minutes is good enough.
  7. Assemble your burger in the order you desire. Let's not joke around, assemble it in the order your demonic tabby cat desired.
